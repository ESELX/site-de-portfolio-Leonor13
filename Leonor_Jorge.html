about me

Leonor Gorjão Jorge, 23

I started drawing when I was a little girl. Portraits, mostly. I loved capturing people´s eyes and emotions on paper. For then on my passion only grew bigger. Nowadays, I love drawing, painting and taking pictures. Photography became a passion of mine as well.

I'm a self-taught artist in all areas.
